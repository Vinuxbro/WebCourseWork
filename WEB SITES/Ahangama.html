Welcome to Ahangama

The Southwestern town of Ahangama is a hidden treasure, home to some of the best surf beaches in Sri Lanka. Uncrowded waves for every level of surfer can be found here, from beginners to experts, with ideal conditions for both long and shortboarding. Kabalana “The Rock” with its perfect A-frame breaks, is one of its most famous swells.

Ahangama is known as one of the best low-key surfing spots in Sri Lanka, making it a wonderful destination for surf enthusiasts. It has some of the best and most consistent surf spots, with great surf breaks making for thrilling fun for both beginners and experienced surfers alike.

Surfing in Ahangama

Sri Lanka is blessed with over 1,400 km of beaches around the island. The Ahangama to Midigama coastal stretch is ideally suited for both beginners and experienced surfers. The beaches and ocean is pristine and considered an amazing and exhilarating experience, by even the most discerning surfer.

While there is a surf season in the South of Sri Lanka (November – April) and in the East (May – October) a good swell is always present at all times. For your safety and enjoyment, it is recommended to heed the advice of the locals at all times.
